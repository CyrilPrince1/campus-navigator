About Us

Campus Navigator develops ethical, inclusive digital solutions that enhance student life. In collaboration with university stakeholders, our team seeks to reduce stress, disorientation, and time-wasting by introducing efficient navigation and scheduling tools tailored to local campus needs.

"Empowering students through intelligent campus technology for academic and social success."

Our Vision

Campus Navigator envisions a future where every student can explore and engage with their campus using real-time smart technology. We aim to reduce disorientation, improve campus safety, and increase productivity through intuitive, accessible platforms.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify campus exploration. We understand that a large university campus can be overwhelming, especially for newcomers. By providing clear directions, detailed location information, and an intuitive interface, we aim to reduce stress and enhance the campus experience for everyone.

Our Goals

  • Interactive Campus Map: A dynamic map showing all key buildings, facilities, and points of interest.
  • Smart Search: Easily find any campus location by name.
  • Turn-by-Turn Directions : Get precise directions for walking, driving, or cycling between any two points on campus, powered by OSRM
  • "My Location" Feature: Instantly pinpoint your current position on the map.
  • Quick Links: Fast access to popular campus destinations.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for seamless use on any device, from smartphones to desktops.

Who We Are

Our team consists of passionate developers and designers dedicated to improving campus life through smart digital innovation. Campus Navigator is designed to make campus navigation seamless, accessible, and efficient for students, staff, and visitors.
